    <h2>ABOUT ME</h2>
    <p>I'm currently pursuing an MBA at the <strong>Asian Institute of Management</strong>, and lately I've been happily obsessing over <strong>corporate and aviation finance</strong> and <strong>data science</strong>.</p>
    <p>I'm an engineer licensed across five continents and a growth-focused leader with <strong>18 years of experience</strong> creating value in telecom, security, commercial aviation, and space. Whether I'm steering projects, programs, and operations or shaping commercial strategy, governance, and solutions, I turn complex challenges into measurable results, including <strong>more than $200 million in project wins</strong> under my portfolio.</p>
    <p>My credentials pair deep technical skill with strategic insight. I hold the European Engineer (EUR ING) title, Professional Engineer (P.E.) licenses in five U.S. states (KY, IL, ID, NV, TX), UK Chartered Engineer (CEng), the Saudi Council of Engineers (SCE) Consultant-Grade Engineer qualification, and the APEC Engineer title. I'm also certified as a Project Management Professional (PMP), PRINCE2 Registered Practitioner and Project Manager, Lean Six Sigma Black Belt, and Certified Scrum Master.</p>
    <p>I stay connected to the global engineering community through active membership in the Royal Aeronautical Society (RAeS) and the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET).</p>
    <p>If you're big on innovation, scale, and meaningful impact, let's connect: <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/kennethvallespin/">linkedin.com/in/kennethvallespin</a>.</p>
